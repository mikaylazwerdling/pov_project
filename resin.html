Toad in Resin
The common European toad is one of the most widespread species on earth and be found in such distant regions as Siberia and North Africa. Despite their small stature, toads can be a fearsome enemy: they secrete a toxin that deters most of their natural predators.

This toad has been preserved in a resin block and makes a unique paperweight. In addition, these specimens are so beautiful that they can also be displayed on their own on a bookshelf or mantle.
